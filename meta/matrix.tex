Rotates $(x_0, y_0, z_0)$, $angle$ degrees around the $(x, y, z)$ pole.

$$
\begin{bmatrix}
		$x^2(1-c)+c$ & $xy(1-c)-zs$ & $xz(1-c)+ys$ & $0$ \\
		$yx(1-c)+zs$ & $y^2(1-c)+c$ & $yz(1-c)-ys$ & $0$ \\
		$zx(1-c)-ys$ & $yz(1-c)+xs$ & $z^2(1-c)+c$ & $0$ \\
		$0$          & $0$          & $0$          & $1$ \\
\end{bmatrix}
\begin{bmatrix}
		$x_0$ \\
		$y_0$ \\
		$z_0$ \\
		$1$   \\
\end{bmatrix}
=
\begin{bmatrix}
		$x_1$ \\
		$y_1$ \\
		$z_1$ \\
		$1$   \\
\end{bmatrix}
$$

$$c = cos(angle)$$
$$s = sin(angle)$$
